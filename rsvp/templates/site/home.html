Winter is Coming...

It's going to be unusually cold in Gilroy this weekend, and all events are outdoors. Low temperatures for this weekend are in the mid-50s, so bring extra layers!

Call Us Old Fashioned

Red fortunately happens to be the traditional wedding color in both Indian and Chinese cultures, making it an easy choice for our wedding attire.

So rather than wearing Western white and black (colors of mourning for both our cultures), we will be in traditional scarlet qipao/magua and sari/kurta outfits.

We welcome you to help us make this a colorful tribute to our dual heritages! Whether that means pulling out that impulse-buy sari or bright-blue button-up, we'll be applauding you all the way.
